# Official Source Selection

## Primary official source profile

- Kyiv-focused official source
- Must cover both air raid / emergency alerts and Kyiv city or regional updates
- Preferred endpoint form: RSS/XML feed
- UI label: Kyiv Official Alerts

## What counts as official

- Government source
- Municipal authority
- Officially linked emergency service or city channel
- Officially verified channels of those institutions

## Fallback official source profile

- National Ukrainian official source
- Used only when the primary source is unavailable or unusable

## Day 1 operating mode

- A temporary semi-manual step is acceptable if needed to keep the demo working
